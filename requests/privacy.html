Privacy Notice

Last updated 8 June 2026

Who we are

This notice explains how Beverley Round Table uses the information you give us when you apply for funding through our donation request form. Beverley Round Table is the data controller for that information. If you have any questions about it, or want to exercise any of the rights below, email us at beverley247@roundtable.org.uk.

What we collect

We only collect what you enter on the form:

  • Your contact details — your name, email address and phone number.
  • About your organisation — its name and, where relevant, a company or charity number.
  • About your request — a summary, the amount requested, who will benefit, the postcode of the activity, and any details of total cost or other funding.
  • Bank details — the account name, sort code and account number you give us to receive a grant, if your request is successful.
  • Declarations — whether anyone connected to the request has a link to a Round Table member, and whether the activity involves children or vulnerable adults.

Why we use it, and our lawful basis

We use your information to assess your request, to contact you about it, to make a payment if it's approved, and to keep proper records of the funding we give out. Our lawful basis is our legitimate interests in running a community grants programme fairly and accountably, together with your consent, given when you submit the form. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us, though we may keep a minimal record where we need to.

Requests involving children or vulnerable adults

If your request involves children or vulnerable adults, we ask only for what we need to consider it responsibly. We keep any such information to a minimum, limit who can see it, and delete it on the same timetable as the rest of your details.

Who can see it

Your information is seen only by the Beverley Round Table members involved in assessing and administering requests. We hold it in Google Workspace and serve our forms through Cloudflare; these providers process the data on our instructions and on our behalf. Your bank details are used solely to make a payment. We do not sell your information or share it for marketing, and we don't share it with anyone else unless we're required to by law.

How long we keep it

We delete your bank details as soon as a grant is paid. We delete applicants' personal details within 12 months of a request being closed. We keep an anonymised financial record — the amount, the type of cause, the area and the date — for our accounts, but this does not identify you.

Where your information is stored

Our providers (Google and Cloudflare) may store or process data on servers outside the UK. Where they do, they operate under recognised safeguards designed to protect your information to UK standards.

Cookies

The donation request form and this page do not set cookies and do not use analytics or tracking.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • ask for a copy of the information we hold about you;
  • ask us to correct anything that's wrong;
  • ask us to delete your information;
  • ask us to restrict or object to how we use it;
  • ask us to provide it in a portable form.

To exercise any of these, email beverley247@roundtable.org.uk. We'll respond within one month.

Complaints

If you're unhappy with how we've handled your information, please tell us first so we can put it right. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Changes to this notice

We may update this notice from time to time. The date at the top shows when it was last changed.
